Information Design

The Information Gathering stage is the foundation of the entire web development process. During this stage, the web developer works with the client or team to understand the purpose of the website and who the target audience is. A web developer will:

  • Information Gathering
  • Strategy
  • Content Writing
  • Design
  • Development
  • Testing
  • Launch
  • Maintance
