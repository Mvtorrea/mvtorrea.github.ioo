WE WANT TO YOU KNOW ABOUT MENTAL DISORDERS AND WHAT YOU SHOULD DO IF YOU HAVE

I made this website about mental disorders to bring attention to an important issue that affects millions of people. Many people do not fully understand mental health, and some may judge others because of misinformation. By creating this website, I want to catch the audience’s attention and help them learn the facts. My goal is to spread awareness, reduce stigma, and encourage people to be more supportive and understanding. Mental health matters, and talking about it openly can make a big difference in people’s lives.
